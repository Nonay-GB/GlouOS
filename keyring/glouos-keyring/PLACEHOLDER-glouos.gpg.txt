PLACEHOLDER - the real glouos.gpg (the exported public key) does not exist yet.

It is created automatically when you run:

    tools/make-signing-key.sh "GlouOS Package Signing" keys@glouos.example

That writes glouos.gpg and fills in glouos-trusted in this folder. After that,
build the package with `makepkg -f` and add it to your [glouos] repo. Until the
key exists, the keyring package cannot be built (by design - there is nothing to
trust yet). Delete this placeholder once glouos.gpg is generated.
